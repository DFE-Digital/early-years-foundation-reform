# Use MADR framework for ADRs

* Status: accepted, supersedes [ADR-0001](0001-record-architecture-decisions.md)

## Context and Problem Statement
Succinctly record architecturally significant technical decisions

## Decision Drivers
* Alignment with other projects
* Familiarity among the team
* Lean, not verbose

## Considered Options
* Original development team adopted [framework described by Michael Nygard](http://thinkrelevance.com/blog/2011/11/15/documenting-architecture-decisions)
* [MADR](https://adr.github.io/madr/)

## Decision Outcome
Switch to MADR
